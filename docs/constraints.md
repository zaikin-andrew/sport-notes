# Constraints

## Life Constraints

- Work and family responsibilities affect training availability.
- Training must be sustainable and not require perfect conditions.
- Some days may require short sessions or plan changes.

## Training Constraints

- Avoid hidden fatigue accumulation.
- Balance running and cycling.
- Do not stack hard run, hard bike, long ride, long run, and leg strength without recovery.
- Monday should normally remain a full rest day.

## Injury Risk Constraints

Monitor:

- shins
- calves
- Achilles
- knees
- hips
- lower back

Do not run through pain that changes mechanics.

## Nutrition Constraints

- Fat loss is desired, but not at the cost of recovery.
- Avoid aggressive dieting during heavy endurance weeks.
- Sweets with coffee may be a trigger and should be tracked.
- Non-alcoholic beer in the evening may be acceptable if it does not harm calories, appetite, or sleep.

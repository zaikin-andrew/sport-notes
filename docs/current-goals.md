# Current Goals

## Primary Goal

Improve long-term health, immune resilience, aerobic fitness, and body composition through sustainable endurance training, mobility, and nutrition.

## Secondary Goals

- Maintain and improve running capacity
- Maintain and improve cycling/gravel endurance
- Reduce excess body fat gradually
- Improve consistency
- Avoid injury and chronic fatigue
- Build a repeatable training system supported by data

## Performance Goals

Update when a race or target event is confirmed.

Potential running goals:

- Improve half-marathon time gradually
- Build toward stronger aerobic pace
- Improve ability to run at lower HR

Potential cycling goals:

- Improve long gravel endurance
- Build stable Z2 power
- Avoid excessive 100+ km rides unless part of a planned block

## Body Composition Goal

Reduce fat mass without compromising training quality, sleep, mood, or immune resilience.

Use gradual deficit and high-protein food structure.

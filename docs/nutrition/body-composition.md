# Body Composition

## Goal

Gradual fat loss while preserving training quality, lean mass, sleep, mood, and immune resilience.

## Metrics To Track

- body weight trend, not single days
- waist measurement
- photos if acceptable
- training performance
- hunger
- sleep
- mood
- recovery

## Rules

- Do not use aggressive calorie deficit during high training load.
- Protein must stay high.
- Carbohydrates should support long and hard sessions.
- Reduce low-value calories before reducing training fuel.
- If performance, sleep, or mood worsens, the deficit may be too aggressive.

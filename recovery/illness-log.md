## Return To Training

Use gradual return:

1. Rest or walking during acute symptoms
2. Easy Z1/Z2 only when symptoms are mild and stable
3. Normal easy training after symptoms improve
4. Intensity only after several normal days

Do not resume hard training immediately after the first better day.

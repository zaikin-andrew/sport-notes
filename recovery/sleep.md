# Sleep

## Sleep Tracking

Track trends, not isolated nights.

Useful fields:

- bedtime
- wake time
- total sleep
- sleep quality
- resting HR
- HRV
- night awakenings
- late food
- alcohol or non-alcoholic beer
- hard training late in day

## Sleep Rules

If sleep is poor for 2+ nights, reduce intensity.

If poor sleep combines with high HR, low HRV, or heavy legs, downgrade training.
